          <h2 class="h2 fw-bold mb-3 text-white">Our Guiding Principles</h2>
          <p>
            S-CORE is built upon core principles that champion inclusivity, efficiency, and innovation within the open source automotive software community.
            Our collaborative framework welcomes contributions from everyone, paving the way for more advanced and robust solutions.
            Below are the guiding principles that steer the S-CORE ecosystem
          </p>
        </div>
        <div class="communities-content mt-5">
          <div class="community-card">
            <img src="images/campus/01_community.svg" alt="Evolve, Everybody Can Join:" />
            <h3>Evolve, Everybody Can Join:</h3>
            <p>
              We recognize the power of collective intelligence and the strength of diverse contributions.
              Everyone is welcome to participate, use, and join S-CORE, fostering a vibrant community united by shared goals and mutual growth.
            </p>
          </div>
          <div class="community-card">
            <img src="images/campus/02_no_reimpl.svg" alt="No Re-Implementation:" />
            <h3>No Re-Implementation:</h3>
            <p>
              Our approach leverages existing solutions to optimize development processes.
              By building on established findings and methodologies, we avoid redundancy, focusing our efforts on crafting innovative and efficient solutions.
            </p>
          </div>
          <div class="community-card">
            <img src="images/campus/03_working_model.svg" alt="Working Model - Shift Left:" />
            <h3>Working Model - Shift Left:</h3>
            <p>
              We adopt a proactive development model that prioritizes early testing and integration,
              ensuring high quality and minimizing risks throughout the software lifecycle.
            </p>
          </div>
          <div class="community-card">
            <img src="images/campus/04_eco_system.svg" alt="S-CORE Ecosystem:" />
            <h3>S-CORE Ecosystem:</h3>
            <p>
              Our ecosystem thrives on collaboration and co-creation across various stakeholders, including universities,
              industry partners, and EU commissions. We encourage dynamic contributions and collective efforts to achieve shared objectives.
            </p>
          </div>
          <div class="community-card">
            <img src="images/campus/05_engage.svg" alt="Engage and Get Involved:" />
            <h3>Engage and Get Involved:</h3>
            <p>
              Join us on this transformative journey and explore opportunities to contribute to S-CORE's evolution. Visit our Get Involved pages for more details,
              and learn about the S-CORE structure by browsing the About Us section for comprehensive insights.
            </p>
          </div>
        </div>
